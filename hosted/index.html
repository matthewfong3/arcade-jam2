ARCADE-JAM

Please use an HTML 5 browser

Instructions

Players 1 and 2 are on the red team. Players 3 and 4 are on the blue team.

Players can shoot projectiles and if they reach the opposite sides of the screen, their team gets a point.

The first team to 3 points wins! Players can also block projectiles that the other team fires.

Controls

W - Move Up
S - Move Down
F - Fire Projectile
Spacebar - Shield
